Weightlifting for sports performance

Why weightlifting is important for sports performance

It is important for sports performance because it can improve strenght, power, endurance and flexibility. Weightlifting should be a part of your training schedule.

Types of weightlifting workouts



  • Hypertrophy
  • Impulse and power
  • Plyometrics
  • Endurance

Hypertrophy workout type


Hypertrophy training is a type of workout that stimulates muscle growth which is the goal of this type workout.
Muscle growth can help with sports performance just as it can decrease sport performance because muscle mass is weight
and it can help you by adding more strenght and power to your body just as it can slow you down because of the added bodyweight.

Impulse and power workout type


Impulse and power training is a type of workout that involves focus on power and impulse which means it is focusing
on strenght in a short period of time. It can really help with sports that have fast strenght usage such as: running, swimming, soccer, football, basketball etc.
